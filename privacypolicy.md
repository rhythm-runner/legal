# Rhythm Runner Privacy Policy

*Last updated: **May 16, 2026***

Rhythm Runner is operated by Rhythm Runners Group. 

## Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Rhythm Runner collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you use the app. This includes information related to run recording, music timelines, routes, AI features, weather, social features, and account functionality. 

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Account Information
We collect information to create and manage your account, including your email address, user ID, authentication information, profile details, and account settings.

### B. Run and Activity Data
When you use Rhythm Runner, we collect run start and end times, duration, distance, pace, timestamps, route and activity events, saved runs, and activity metrics.

### C. Location and Route Data
If you grant permission, we collect your precise GPS location while recording a run. This tracking occurs in the background (including when the app is minimized or your screen is locked), but *only* while you are actively recording a run. This includes GPS coordinates, route paths, timestamps, pace and location-related data, map visualizations, and beginning/end route hiding where available. 

This location data is used to map routes, associate music with locations and moments, calculate activity metrics, retrieve contextual run information, and create run memories.

### D. Health and Fitness Data
If you grant permission, Rhythm Runner may access Apple Health / HealthKit or fitness-related data such as workouts, distance, duration, pace, heart rate, cadence, calories, and related running metrics.

**HealthKit and fitness data is used only for app functionality and is not used for advertising, marketing, data brokerage, or tracking across apps or websites.** You can manage these permissions in Apple Health or your iOS Settings.

### E. Music Data
We collect music data to associate your listening experience with your runs. This includes Apple Music / MusicKit data where authorized or available, track identifiers, track titles, artist names, album names, album artwork references, playback timestamps, music service identifiers, and song timing during a run.

*Note regarding Spotify:* Spotify account connection is not currently supported for general users, but the app may display external links to Spotify songs where available. Rhythm Runner does not currently collect Spotify account authentication data from general users.

### F. Weather Data
Rhythm Runner may process your run time, approximate or precise location/route context, and related activity context to retrieve weather information, such as temperature, conditions, wind, humidity, and weather summary or metrics. Apple WeatherKit and/or Visual Crossing may be used to provide weather data associated with your runs.

### G. Photos and User Content
We collect content you choose to provide, including photos, captions, run names, profile information, comments (if applicable), tags/vibes, reports, and other content you choose to upload or create. Please note that photos or content may reveal location, people, or personal details, and you are responsible for choosing what you upload and share.

### H. Social and Safety Information
We collect information related to your social interactions, including friend relationships, shared run visibility, hide-from-friends state, route beginning/end hiding settings where available, blocks, reports, moderation information, and related interactions.

### I. AI Feature Data
Rhythm Runner includes live AI features. We may process relevant app data to provide AI-assisted features, such as run summaries, reflections, recommendations, visualizations, generated images, and insights. Relevant app data may include run data, route/location context, music data, weather context, user-provided prompts or captions, photos selected by the user, and related app data. Service providers for these features may include Stability AI and Gemini / Google AI.

### J. Diagnostics, Analytics, and Device Information
We collect crash logs, error reports, app version, device type, operating system version, performance data, product interaction data, approximate usage patterns, and security or abuse-prevention data. We use services such as Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to process this information. This information is used for reliability, debugging, performance, safety, security, abuse prevention, and app improvement.

### K. Contacts and Friend Discovery
With your permission, Rhythm Runner may access your device contacts (via iOS Contacts) to help you find friends who use Rhythm Runner. Contact information may include email addresses, phone numbers, and names if available. This information is used solely for friend discovery, matching contacts against Rhythm Runner users, and app functionality. We do not permanently store your raw contacts, nor do we sell or share them with data brokers or advertising networks.

## 2. How We Use Information

Rhythm Runner uses the information we collect to:
*   Create, manage, and secure accounts.
*   Record, save, display, and sync runs.
*   Map routes.
*   Connect music to moments during runs.
*   Calculate and display running metrics.
*   Retrieve and display weather context.
*   Provide music timelines and run memories.
*   Provide AI-assisted summaries, reflections, recommendations, images, and insights.
*   Support social features such as profiles, friends, shared runs, blocking, reporting, and moderation.
*   Help users find friends and match contacts against Rhythm Runner users to support friend discovery.
*   Provide support.
*   Improve performance, reliability, security, and user experience.
*   Prevent abuse and enforce our Terms.
*   Comply with legal obligations.

## 3. How We Share Information

### A. No Sale of Personal Information
**We do not sell your personal information.**

### B. Service Providers
Rhythm Runner may share information with trusted providers that help operate the app, including services for authentication, hosting, database, file storage, analytics, crash reporting, search, maps, weather, music integrations, AI and content generation, customer support, and security. 

Provider examples may include:
*   Firebase / Google Cloud (including Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics)
*   Algolia
*   Mapbox
*   Apple Music / MusicKit
*   Apple WeatherKit
*   Apple Health / HealthKit (where authorized by the user)
*   Visual Crossing
*   Stability AI
*   Cyanite
*   Gemini / Google AI
*   Spotify (external links where available, but not general Spotify account connection)

These providers process information as needed to provide their services. Note that contacts are not sold, shared with data brokers, or used for advertising. Contact information may be processed by trusted service providers only as needed to provide friend discovery and app functionality.

### C. Connected Services
If you authorize or use Apple Music, HealthKit, mapping, weather, or other connected services, information may be exchanged as needed for those features and governed by those services’ own terms and privacy policies.

### D. Other Users
If you share runs or use social features, other users may see information depending on your app settings and features, such as your profile, shared runs, route previews, music activity, photos, captions, comments, and other content. You can control your visibility, including hiding an entire run from friends and hiding the beginning or end of a route where available.

### E. Safety, Legal, and Security
We may share information to comply with the law, enforce our Terms, prevent fraud or abuse, respond to reports, and protect the rights, safety, and security of Rhythm Runner, our users, or others.

### F. Business Transfers
If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

## 4. No Tracking / Advertising

*   Rhythm Runner does not sell personal information or contacts.
*   Rhythm Runner does not share personal information or contacts with data brokers.
*   Rhythm Runner does not use HealthKit data, contacts, precise location data, or music listening data for third-party advertising.
*   Rhythm Runner does not use personal information to track users across apps or websites for advertising purposes.

---

## 5. Your Choices and Controls

You can manage your information and permissions in several ways:
*   **Location permission:** Manage in iOS Settings.
*   **Photos permission:** Manage in iOS Settings.
*   **Contacts permission:** Manage in iOS Settings. Disabling Contacts permission may limit friend discovery features but will not prevent core run recording.
*   **HealthKit permission:** Manage in Apple Health or iOS Settings.
*   **Notifications:** Manage in iOS Settings.
*   **Music service permissions:** Manage in the respective service settings where applicable.
*   **App Settings:** Manage your account, sharing, blocking/reporting, run visibility, hiding entire runs from friends, and deleting your account directly within the Rhythm Runner app settings.
*   **Route privacy:** Manage route beginning/end hiding controls where available.

Please note that disabling certain permissions may limit the app's features.

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as needed to provide the app, maintain your account, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce our Terms, prevent abuse, and maintain security.

You can delete your account at any time in the app settings. Account deletion is designed to delete or disable access to associated account data, your profile, runs, uploaded content, and related app data, subject to limited exceptions. Some information may remain temporarily in backups, logs, or security records. Additionally, some shared content, reports, moderation records, or references may remain where necessary for safety, integrity, legal compliance, or preserving other users’ experience.

Health data stored in Apple Health is controlled through Apple Health or iOS settings. If Rhythm Runner saves data to Apple Health, deleting your account from Rhythm Runner may not automatically delete the separate Apple Health records.
